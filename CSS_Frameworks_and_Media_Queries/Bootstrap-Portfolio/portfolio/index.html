About Me

Developed several new methods for buying and selling action figures worldwide. Spent several years creating marketing channels for etch-a-sketches for farmers. Spent college summers researching velcro in Pensacola, FL. Spent two years testing the market for clip-on ties in Gainesville, FL. Spent 2001-2008 buying and selling action figures on Wall Street. Spent 2002-2009 lecturing about trumpets for the underprivileged.

Spent 2002-2007 building crayon art in New York, NY. Had moderate success managing Mr. Potato Heads in Salisbury, MD. Have a strong interest in getting my feet wet with shaving cream in Fort Walton Beach, FL. Had a brief career buying and selling sheep on the black market.
